## 💡 AI Tips & Techniques

### 🟢 [OpenAI's New Prompting Guide: From Step-Driven to Result-Driven](https://theaicareerlab.com/blog/openai-chatgpt-prompting-guide-july-2026)
**Source**: OpenAI Academy [🏢 Official] [🟩 T1] | **Date**: 2026-07-13
