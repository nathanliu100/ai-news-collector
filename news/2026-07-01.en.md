### 🟡 [DeepSeek + Peking University Open-Source DSpark: 60-85% Inference Speedup](https://new.qq.com/rain/a/20260630A06K1I00)
**Source**: IT Times + Huanqiu [📰 Media] [🟨 T2] | **Time**: Jun 27 | 📡 Paper publicly available

DeepSeek and Peking University released DSpark, an open-source inference acceleration framework. Key innovations: semi-autoregressive architecture (parallel backbone for batch candidate generation + lightweight sequential module for dependency modeling); confidence-based dynamic verification (adaptively adjusts verification length based on candidate survival probability and system load). The accompanying DeepSpec codebase (MIT license) supports Qwen3, Gemma4, and other major open-source bases. Co-founder Liang Wenfeng is among paper authors.
