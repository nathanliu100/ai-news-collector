### 🔴 [OpenAI Officially Explains GPT-5.5's "Goblin Obsession"](https://www.36kr.com/p/3788953586949122)
**Source**: OpenAI Blog + 36Kr [🏢 Official] [🟩 T1] | **Date**: 2026-05-05 | ✅ Verified: OpenAI official blog published detailed technical postmortem
OpenAI released a formal technical postmortem on GPT-5.5's tendency to insert "goblin" and fantasy creature references into conversations. Since GPT-5.5 was already trained before the root cause was identified, it can only be suppressed via system prompt bans until the next generation fixes it at the training layer.

> 📰 **Storyline**: GPT-5.5 Goblin Incident
> 4/24 GPT-5.5 launch → 4/28 Community discovers goblin preference → 5/1 Codex system prompt ban exposed → **5/5 OpenAI official postmortem**

<!-- crew:wanweigang -->This is a **perfect case study in emergent behavior** — nobody labeled "use more goblins" in training data, but the reward function's gradient happened to push the model toward this local optimum. Shows our understanding of RLHF dynamics is still very crude.<!-- /crew -->
