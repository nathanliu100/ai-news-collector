### 🔴 [Google Open-Sources DiffusionGemma: Text Diffusion Architecture, 4x Local Inference Speed](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/developers-tools/diffusion-gemma-faster-text-generation/)
**Source**: Google Official Blog [🏢 Official] [🟩 T1] | **Date**: 2026-06-11 | ✅ Verified: Google blog + IT Home + multiple outlets
Google DeepMind releases DiffusionGemma, **pioneering a text diffusion generation paradigm** — generates 256 tokens in parallel then iteratively refines, rather than sequential token-by-token generation. 26B MoE architecture (only 3.8B active at inference), runs on 18GB VRAM. H100: 1000+ tokens/s, RTX 5090: 700+ tokens/s — **~4x faster than autoregressive models**. Apache 2.0 open-source, jointly optimized with NVIDIA. Output quality still below standard Gemma 4, positioned for experimental/speed-critical scenarios. [💎 Dept Related]
<!-- crew:elon -->This is **real architectural innovation** — not stacking tricks on autoregressive, but swapping out the entire engine. 4x speed in local inference is a qualitative change. Google choosing Apache 2.0 open-source is a smart move — ecosystem moats are worth more than models.<!-- /crew -->
<!-- crew:jobs -->26 billion parameters running on 18GB VRAM makes **"AI on your laptop" go from slogan to reality**. Speed is there but quality isn't — classic v1 product: right direction, needs polish.<!-- /crew -->
<!-- crew:wanweigang -->Diffusion models for text isn't new, but previous attempts stayed at small scale. DiffusionGemma proves **this path works at 26B scale**. More interesting: bidirectional attention makes it naturally suited for non-linear tasks — Sudoku, code infilling, protein sequences.<!-- /crew -->
<!-- crew:tim -->1000 tokens/s locally means **real-time subtitles and real-time copywriting iteration become possible**. In video post-production, text layer generation speed has always been a bottleneck — this directly unblocks it.<!-- /crew -->
<!-- crew:mrbeast -->Open-source plus consumer GPU compatible — **the barrier for creators just dropped again**. But I care about when quality catches up. 4x faster but lower quality copy is useless for content people.<!-- /crew -->
