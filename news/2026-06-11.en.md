### 🟡 [Google Releases DiffusionGemma: Open-Source Text Diffusion, 4x Local Inference Speed](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/rtx-ai-garage-local-gemma-diffusion)
**Source**: Google DeepMind + NVIDIA official blogs [🏢 Official] [🟩 T1] | **Date**: 2026-06-10 | ✅ Verified: Google blog + NVIDIA blog + 36Kr
Google DeepMind releases DiffusionGemma, replacing token-by-token generation with parallel 256-token diffusion. Built on Gemma 4 architecture (26B MoE / 3.8B active params), achieving 1000+ tokens/s on H100 and 700+ on RTX 5090. Only 18GB VRAM quantized. Apache 2.0 license. Google acknowledges quality is below standard Gemma 4 — positioned as experimental. [💎 Department Related]
