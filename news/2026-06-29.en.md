**Source**: OpenAI Official [🏢 Official] [🟩 T1] | **Date**: 2026-06-27 | ✅ Verified: OpenAI blog + WSJ + TechWeb

### 🔴 [DeepSeek Open-Sources DSpark: 85% Faster Inference on Live Traffic](https://github.com/deepseek-ai/DeepSpec)
**Source**: 36Kr / Jiqizhixin / Yicai [📰 Media] [🟩 T1] | **Date**: 2026-06-27 | ✅ Verified: GitHub repo + paper PDF + Liang Wenfeng authorship
DeepSeek + Peking University released DSpark speculative decoding framework. Core innovations: semi-autoregressive generation + confidence-scheduled verification. Deployed on V4-Flash/V4-Pro live traffic; single-user generation speed up 60-85% at equivalent throughput. Open-sourced DeepSpec full-stack codebase (MIT). Also compatible with Qwen3, Gemma4. First major output after DeepSeek's ¥50B funding round.

<!-- crew:wanweigang -->The most interesting detail: the confidence scheduler dynamically allocates compute based on real-time load—**only verifying high-confidence tokens under pressure**. This isn't pure algorithm; it's systems engineering wisdom.<!-- /crew -->
